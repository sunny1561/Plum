## Evaluation Notes

### Financial calculation order (TC010)
The system applies network discount first, then co-pay, matching the exact calculation in the expected outcome:
`₹4,500 × (1 - 0.20) = ₹3,600 → ₹3,600 × (1 - 0.10) = ₹3,240`

### Per-claim limit and dental claims (TC006)
The general `per_claim_limit: ₹5,000` does not apply to DENTAL and VISION claims, which use their own category `sub_limit` (dental: ₹10,000). This is necessary for TC006 to produce PARTIAL rather than REJECTED — the total bill (₹12,000) exceeds the general limit but the dental sub_limit (₹10,000) governs instead. The approved amount (₹8,000 for root canal only) falls within the dental sub_limit.

### Exclusion vs. waiting period precedence (TC012)
Exclusions are evaluated before condition-specific waiting periods. Obesity/bariatric treatment is both in the exclusion list AND has a 365-day waiting period. The system correctly surfaces EXCLUDED_CONDITION (permanent) rather than WAITING_PERIOD (temporary), because a claim for an excluded treatment is never coverable regardless of waiting period.

### Submission deadline
The 30-day submission deadline from `submission_rules` is enforced at the API intake layer (where the submission timestamp is available), not inside `PolicyEngine.evaluate()`. This prevents false rejections when processing historical test-case data.
